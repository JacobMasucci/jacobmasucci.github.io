My Other Interests

When I was young, I was never interested in music. Yet, as I grow older, I have come to appreciate a wide range of genres.

Some of my favorite bands include The Doors, Alice in Chains, Pink Floyd, Kid Cudi, xxxtentacion, Eve 6, Greenday, Everclear, X, and Phil Collins.

Some of the most influential role models in my life include Jordan Peterson, Rollo Tomassi and Stefan Molyneux.

The novels that have had the deepest impact on my life are George Orwell's "1984", and "The Remains of the Day" by Kazuo Ishiguro

Many of the ideas shared in 1984, reflect the struggles of security and surveillance that occur today. With increasing abundance of technology and information it has become commonplace for peoples basic rights to be violated in the name of "security". Edward Snowden stands as a living example of how powerful and in control the surveillance state is.
